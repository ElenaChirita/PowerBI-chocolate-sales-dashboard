# Chocolate Sales Dashboard 🍫📊

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze chocolate sales performance across multiple business areas.

The report focuses on revenue trends, profitability, customer insights, and product/category performance.

## Dashboard Pages


### 1. Sales Analysis

This page focuses on overall revenue performance, growth trends, market contribution, and store-level sales results.

Key elements include:

* Total Revenue and Revenue Growth %
* Revenue vs Target Progress Tracking
* Remaining Revenue to Target
* Average Monthly Revenue
* Revenue Trend: Current Year vs Last Year
* Total Revenue by Month and Country
* YoY % Performance by Category
* Store Sales Performance Comparison

Main insights:

* Revenue remained stable with slight growth during the selected period
* Average monthly revenue exceeded $1M
* Some categories outperformed while others declined year-over-year
* Multi-country sales contribution was balanced across markets
* Store-level analysis highlighted both top and underperforming locations


### 2. Profit Analysis

This page focuses on profitability performance, cost efficiency, margin strength, and category/store contribution to total profit.

Key elements include:

* Total Profit and Profit Growth %
* Profit vs Target Progress Tracking
* Total Cost
* Profit Margin %
* Cost vs Unit Price Comparison
* Profit Share by Category
* Monthly Profit Trend
* Store Profit Performance Comparison

Main insights:

* Profit remained stable with slight growth during the selected period
* Profit margin remained strong at 40%
* Selling prices stayed above average unit costs across categories
* Praline and White chocolate categories were the largest profit contributors
* Monthly profit performance remained stable with moderate fluctuations
* Store-level analysis highlighted both top and underperforming locations


### 3. Customer Analysis

This page focuses on customer demographics, geographic reach, loyalty participation, and acquisition trends.

Key elements include:

* Total Customers and Countries Served
* Loyalty Members and Loyalty Rate %
* Customer Distribution by Country
* Gender Distribution
* Customer Acquisition by Month
* Customer Age Distribution

Main insights:

* Customer base is evenly split by gender
* Loyalty program participation exceeds 50%
* Customers are distributed across 6 countries
* Monthly customer acquisition remained stable throughout the period
